# Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your information.

## Information We Collect
- **Name**
- **Surname**
- **Email Address**

## Purpose of Data Collection
Your personal information will be used solely for:
- Sending announcements related to the event _HackUDC_.
- Informing you about future editions of the same event.

## Data Storage
Your data will be securely stored and will not be shared with third parties. We will retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes stated above or as required by law.

## Your Rights
Under the GDPR, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:
- **Access**: You can request access to your personal data.
- **Rectification**: You can request correction of any incorrect or incomplete data.
- **Erasure**: You can request deletion of your personal data.
- **Restriction**: You can request restriction of processing of your personal data.
- **Objection**: You can object to the processing of your personal data.
- **Portability**: You can request a copy of your personal data in a commonly used electronic format.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at [hackudc@gpul.org](mailto:hackudc@gpul.org).

## Contact
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data processing practices, please contact us at [hackudc@gpul.org](mailto:hackudc@gpul.org).
